<a name="MEETINGS"></a>
<font size="+2"><U>Meetings</U></font>

<p>
Lectures will be available in three different ways: in-person (details below),
a Zoom meeting, and a recording, posted to Panopto.  <b>Everyone</b> is free to
use any one of these methods, at any time.

<p>
<b>In-Person</b>

<p>
Our class will not be meeting in person at the beginning of the semester.
However, in-person meetings are planned for later in the semster.  When they
begin, we will meet at the following location:

<p>Mon,Wed 3:00-4:15 pm - Meinel Optical Sciences 410

<p>The class will be divided into 2 cohorts; you will only be allowed to
attend class (at most) once per week - when your cohort meets.

<p>
<b>Zoom</b>

<p>
Our meetings will be live-streamed using a Zoom meeting.  Eventually; this will
be a stream of the in-person meeting; for now, I will host this from elsewhere.
I <b>strongly encourage</b> everyone to attend the Zoom meeting, if you are
unable to attend the in-person lecture.

Please use this link to join the Zoom meeting: <a href="https://arizona.zoom.us/j/93984017094">https://arizona.zoom.us/j/93984017094</a>.

<p>If you have any trouble connecting to the Zoom chat, we have detailed info
<a href="zoom_details.txt">here</a>.

<p>
<b>Panopto</b>

<p>
If you are unable to participate with the meeting when it happens (or, if you
want to review the meeting later), recordings of the Zoom meetings will be
posted to Panopto.  You can access Panopto by logging into this class on D2L,
then clicking UA Tools->Panopto.
